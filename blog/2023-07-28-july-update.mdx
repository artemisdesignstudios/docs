## Paragon

- Manufacturing Status: **Complete**
- Shipping Status: Most vendors have either completed shipping the GB units or are nearly finished. DailyClack is yet to begin
shipping Paragon GB orders. Extras at CandyKeys and iLumkb are live.

## January

- Manufacturing Status: **In Progress**
    - Polycarbonate cases are almost finished.
    - Aluminum cases have been sandblasted and anodized.
    - Weights (case/wrist rest) are currently being sandblasted.
    - Carrying case and microfiber cloth samples are in production. They should be sent to us for review by the end of the month.
- PCB Status: **In Progress**
    - PCB prototyping was delayed due to manufacturer holidays last month. Prototypes should be completed soon and sent to us for review.
